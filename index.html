PROJECT OVERVIEW & SCOPE

₹8,000 ₹5,400

Donaro is a mobile-first donation platform that rewards real-world social impact. Users can donate food, clothes, blood, books, or essentials. Each donation is verified with proof images (selfie + item photo) and geo-tagged with timestamps. Verified donations are then directly rewarded with money payouts through UPI/Bank transfers. This proposal covers app development, verification features, branding, testing, and deployment.

What's Included

  • Cross-platform Mobile App (Android + iOS), User Authentication (Google/OTP)
  • Donation submission with photo proof & GPS tagging
  • Direct money payout integration (UPI/Bank), Fraud detection & security layer
  • Logo & Brand Kit Design, Testing, QA, and Deployment
  • Clean modern UI with purple-blue branding

COST OPTIMIZATION STRATEGY

By removing admin dashboard & credits wallet, we optimized the budget while keeping core donation verification, money payout, fraud prevention, branding, and testing intact. This ensures maximum impact at minimum cost.
